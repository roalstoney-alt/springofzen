# SPRING OF ZEN

## A Human Harbor for the Agent Era

---

## Why We Exist

For centuries, human progress was defined by our ability to build tools.

We built machines to reduce labor.
We built computers to process information.
We built networks to connect the world.
We built AI to assist thinking.

Now a new era is emerging.

An era where intelligent agents can perform more and more of the work once reserved for humans.

The question is no longer:

"How do we save time?"

The question becomes:

"What should we do with the time that has been returned to us?"

## The Problem

Modern platforms optimize for attention.

Infinite feeds.
Infinite notifications.
Infinite stimulation.

Yet more people report:

- Anxiety
- Burnout
- Loneliness
- Meaninglessness

The problem is not lack of information.

The problem is lack of direction.

## Our Belief

Humans do not only need productivity.

Humans need:

- Reflection
- Recovery
- Creation
- Connection
- Purpose

We believe the next generation of digital spaces will not compete for attention.

They will help people navigate their state.

## Human Harbor

SpringOfZen is an experiment.

A Human Harbor.

A place where people can:

- Pause
- Reflect
- Recover
- Explore
- Create

In a world increasingly optimized by machines.

## What We Study

We explore:

- Human attention
- Human recovery
- Emotional regulation
- Creative states
- Meaningful use of time
- Human-Agent collaboration

## What We Are Building

Not another feed.

Not another social network.

Not another productivity app.

We are building:

A Human State Navigation System.

## Long-Term Question

When machines can do almost everything else,

Where should human attention go?

This is the question we are exploring.

Together.
