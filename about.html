Manifesto

A quiet threshold for the Agent Era.

For centuries, human progress was defined by our ability to build tools. We built machines to reduce labor, computers to process information, networks to connect the world, and AI to assist thinking.

Now a new era is emerging: intelligent agents can perform more and more of the work once reserved for humans.

The question is no longer, "How do we save time?"

The question becomes, "What should we do with the time that has been returned to us?"

SpringOfZen is not another feed.

Modern platforms optimize for attention. Infinite feeds, notifications, and stimulation create more motion, but not always more direction. SpringOfZen exists for reflection, recovery, creation, connection, and purpose.

What We Are Building

SpringOfZen is an experiment in Human State Navigation: a place where people can pause, reflect, recover, explore, and create in a world increasingly optimized by machines.

What We Study

  • Human attention
  • Human recovery
  • Emotional regulation
  • Creative states
  • Meaningful use of time
  • Human-agent collaboration

North Star

If a feature does not help a visitor pause, reflect, recover, create, connect, or return, we do not build it.

The goal is not a successful website. The goal is to create a place people miss when they leave.
