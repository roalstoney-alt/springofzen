Manifesto

A Human Harbor for the Agent Era.

SpringOfZen exists to explore one question: when machines can do more and more of the work, where should human attention go?

We are not building an AI startup, a music website, a meditation platform, a productivity tool, a content factory, or a social network.

We are building a place where people can pause, reflect, recover, create, reconnect, and return.

Build a place people miss when they leave.

Not a website people visit once. Not a feed. Not a dashboard. A Harbor.

The Rule

If a feature does not help a visitor pause, reflect, recover, create, connect, or return, we do not build it.

Build fewer things. Create deeper experiences.

The Harbor Model

  • Beauty: this place feels different.
  • Meaning: this feels relevant.
  • Participation: I am part of this.
  • Research: observations after trust exists.
